Abstract
========

Yao-Hong Liu received his Ph.D. degree from National Taiwan University, Taiwan, in 2009.
Since 2010, he joined imec, the Netherlands. His current position is
Principal Membership of Technical Staff, and he is leading the development
of the ultra-low power (ULP) RFIC design. His research focuses are
energy-efficient digital-intensive RF transceivers and radar for IoT and healthcare applications.
He currently serves as a technical program committee of IEEE ISSCC and RFIC symposium.
